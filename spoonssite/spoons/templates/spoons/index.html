BigSpoon:

A spoons tracker for those with no spoons to waste.

What started as a simple story has evolved into a new way of expressing chronic illness in a way that even small children can understand.

We aim to create a space that will allow people of all ages and illnesses to track their spoons, both in a positive (couting up) way and in a negative (counting down) way.

Join today to discover a new way of tracking your daily energy expenditures and work toward a better, more stable future.
